In the wake of the coronavirus pandemic, the Bank of Baroda has released a list of important numbers for its customers and account holders. Customers can opt to use banking services from home in order to comply with COVID-19 regulations at bank branches. Bank of Baroda assists its customers. 24 hours a day, seven days a week by easy and convenient methods as a result it has announced the following banking facilities and numbers.

To avail WhatsApp banking services you send a message at 8433888777 from your WhatsApp account. Blocking debit card, checking account balance, checking cheque status, interest rates and services, and mini statements are some of the Whatsapp banking services that can be availed without visiting your nearest bank branch.
